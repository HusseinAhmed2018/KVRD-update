Situated within the elite Golden Square development in New Cairo, Sidewalk is a contemporary retail destination which is the focal point of the entire community. Strategically located in one of the highest population density areas in New Cairo and surrounded by high-end residential projects, Sidewalk is very near to the American University in Cairo and serves as the go-to meeting place for university students, young families, and employees of surrounding businesses. With a total area of 6100 square meters, the Sidewalk strip mall is complemented by outdoor entertainment facilities and open air dining destinations. Sidewalk is a truly a destination for all to enjoy.
