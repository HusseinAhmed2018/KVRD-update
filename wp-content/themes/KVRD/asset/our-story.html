GET TO KNOW US

KVRD is the brainchild of professional founders, boasting 15 years of extensive expertise in catering to the diversified needs of the real estate industry in Egypt. Building on a successful development legacy that has gained the trust of varied clients in the field, KVRD was launched as the perfect solution, combining real estate consultancy with development to serve as a selective developer catering to targeted communities, as well as an investment advisor.
